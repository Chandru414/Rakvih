Training & Placements

Training, Placement and Development


At RAKVIH Solutions Pvt. Ltd., we believe in nurturing talent, empowering professionals, and bridging the gap between education and industry requirements. Our goal is to equip individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to thrive in today’s fast-paced professional world. Our specialized programs cover both IT and non-IT domains, ensuring a holistic approach to career development.


Comprehensive Training Programs

Our training programs are designed to cater to both fresh graduates and working professionals who are seeking to advance or switch careers. We offer a wide range of industry-aligned courses that cover core technical and non-technical competencies, with a focus on hands-on learning and real-world applications.


IT Domain Training


⯈ Software Development & Programming: Learn programming languages like Python, Java, C++, JavaScript, and frameworks such as Node.js and React.

⯈ Data Science & Analytics: Equip yourself with data analysis, machine learning, and AI skills. Master tools like Python, R, Tableau, and Power BI.

⯈ Cloud Computing & Cybersecurity: Understand cloud platforms like AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud while enhancing your knowledge of network security, ethical hacking, and risk management.

⯈ Web & Mobile App Development: Develop modern websites and mobile apps using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flutter, and other development frameworks.

⯈ DevOps & Automation: Learn the integration of development and operations to streamline the software delivery process, using tools like Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes.

Non-IT Domain Training


⯈ Business Administration & Management: Gain insights into business strategy, operations, leadership, and team management. Learn the art of decision-making and efficient resource allocation.

⯈ Digital Marketing: Learn SEO, SEM, social media marketing, content creation, and email marketing to drive business growth and customer engagement.

⯈ Finance & Accounting: Understand financial planning, risk management, investment analysis, and corporate accounting practices to stay ahead in the finance industry.

⯈ Human Resources & Talent Management: Master the best practices in hiring, onboarding, employee engagement, and compliance with labor laws.

⯈ Sales & Marketing: Develop critical skills in sales techniques, market analysis, customer relationship management (CRM), and negotiation strategies.

Holistic Development Approach

At RAKVIH Solutions, we believe that career success extends beyond technical expertise. Our development programs emphasize the importance of soft skills and personal growth. This helps candidates stand out not just for their knowledge, but for their ability to collaborate, communicate, and lead in the workplace.


⯈ Soft Skills Training:We offer courses on communication skills, emotional intelligence, time management, teamwork, negotiation, and leadership.

⯈ Personality Development:Our experts provide personalized guidance to improve your confidence, public speaking, and presentation skills, preparing you to excel in any professional setting.

⯈ Career Mentoring & Coaching:Our experienced mentors offer one-on-one career coaching, providing strategic advice on career paths, industry trends, and skill development.

Hands-on Learning & Certifications

We focus on an experiential learning approach, where students are engaged in live projects, case studies, and simulations that mirror real industry challenges.


⯈ Live Projects: Get the chance to work on real-world projects with the guidance of experienced mentors. This allows you to apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios, which is highly valued by employers.

⯈ Internship Opportunities: Our partnerships with industry leaders enable us to offer internship placements that provide real work experience, helping you transition seamlessly into full-time roles.

⯈ Globally Recognized Certifications: Our certification programs are designed to validate your skills and boost your marketability. We offer certifications from leading organizations in both IT and non-IT fields, ensuring your qualifications are recognized worldwide.

Placement Assistance & Career Support

We understand that landing the right job is as important as learning new skills. Our placement cell works tirelessly to ensure that our candidates secure fulfilling career opportunities. Whether you're a fresh graduate or a working professional looking to switch industries, we provide full support throughout your job search process.


⯈ Resume Building: Our experts help you craft a professional resume that highlights your skills, experience, and accomplishments, making you stand out to potential employers.

⯈ Interview Preparation: We provide mock interviews with industry professionals to help you build confidence and perfect your interview techniques. You'll receive feedback on your performance, so you can continue to improve.

⯈ Job Market Insights: Stay informed about the latest trends and opportunities in your field with regular updates from our team. We provide insights into high-demand roles, required skills, and salary expectations.

⯈ Guaranteed Placement Programs: For select programs, we offer placement guarantees. This means that we work with you until you secure a position in your desired field, providing ongoing job search support.

Corporate Training & Development

We also offer a range of corporate training solutions tailored to meet the needs of businesses across industries. Whether you need to upskill your team, onboard new hires, or offer leadership development programs, we have you covered.


⯈ Custom Training Solutions: Our team works closely with your organization to design custom training programs that meet your business goals. From technical upskilling to leadership development, our solutions are designed to increase productivity and employee engagement.

⯈ Leadership Development Programs: We offer targeted programs to develop the next generation of leaders in your organization. Our courses cover topics such as decision-making, conflict resolution, and strategic thinking, helping to prepare your employees for management roles.

⯈ Workshops and Seminars: We conduct workshops and seminars on emerging trends, technologies, and skills that are critical for staying competitive in today’s market. These can be conducted on-site or virtually, depending on your organization’s preferences.
