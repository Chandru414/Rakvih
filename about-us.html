RAKVIH Began its Business Operation as Consulting Service to company in 2020 March & got Registered in March 2022, Mainly Focusing on high quality and cost effective business process implementation services. We are advancing in a tremendous pace with involvement of skilled and experienced people working in the organization. RAKVIH is currently doing business in Government, Banking and other Telecom MNC’s.

RAKVIH Solutions Pvt. Ltd. manages recruitment for revenue cycle management and healthcare companies. Our partner-focused approach means that you can focus on your job role, not just your position. We are uniquely qualified to lead clients, partners and customers on a growth path in changing industry standards and costs. We facilitate strategic results for our clients that are customized to suit them with real-time analytics and an expert team on hand.

RAKVIH Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was founded with the goal of providing quality services to help our clients do business better. We are experts in business process outsourcing and have been instrumental in bringing BPO to the city. Our team is well positioned, and we partner with healthcare organizations and other IT/NON-IT companies with constant reengineering and responsiveness to changing client needs. We set industry benchmarks for process standardization, which allows us to partner with organizations across the world who want access to our expertise in BPO.

From humble beginnings, RAKVIH Solutions Pvt. Ltd has grown steadily since its inception. We have placed 3,000+ candidates in different domains and ranges in Pan India. The phenomenal growth of RAKVIH Solutions can be attributed to the opportunities that the organization presents not just to its clients but also to all candidates. With RAKVIH Solutions, all associates have also grown. Since its inception, through its functioning and through continuous innovation, RAKVIH Solutions has proven that it is in the direction of the future that it heads in. A strong foundation of continual innovation and courage to implement revolutionary ideas fueled by best minds in industry have been underlying driving forces behind this organization’s success.
